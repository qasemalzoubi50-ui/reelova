What is REELOVA?

REELOVA is a content publishing platform that helps creators produce and distribute short-drama videos across major social media platforms in multiple languages.

For TikTok, REELOVA uses the video.upload (draft) flow — videos are delivered to the creator's TikTok inbox, where the creator reviews the content and chooses to publish manually from inside the TikTok app. The creator stays in full control of what goes live. No data from TikTok users is collected or shared with third parties.

About This App

REELOVA is a private developer application owned and operated by a single creator. It does not offer public sign-up, does not collect personal data from platform users, and acts solely as an automation layer for the app owner's own accounts.

All content uploaded through this application is created by and belongs to the app owner. Platform API access is used exclusively for content management on the owner's own channels.
